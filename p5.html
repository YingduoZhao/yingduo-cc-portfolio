CCLab assignment #5 ptical Illusion

Description

This sketch is a moving image of a wallpainting that can interact with the mouse. When the mouse appeared on the picture, the background of the sketch will change, as if the dear in the center is blinking. The circles moving behind it's body are different patterns tooken from the wall paintings from Dunhuang, China. This sketch is done by P5 Javascript.

Design Process

The above image is the process of my design. This image consists of several parts, each part is layered individually, and done by copy& paste and rotation tools in Adobe Illustrator, as you can see in the image.

After finishing all the works on illustrator, I uploaded the png files that I designed in to P5 Javascript, and employed it into my stetch design.

The inspiration of my design is the Dunhuang敦煌 Wallpaintings that appeared on the celling of the caves and a Bhudism story about a magic deer that can change it's color thay was drawn in one of the walls in the caves of Dunhuang. The caves of Dunhhuang were located near The Silk Road. During antient times, the commercial activities happened there helped China and countries that close to it got new goods, prospered the Bhuddism arounf the areas, and developed it's unique style of decorative arts around the city.

The patterns are circulating, representing the river of the slowly moving of history and the lives of the offerers of the cave arets.

Reflection

    5. ·translating something from my cultural background into digital art does impact some of it's meaning. The most obvious thinng being impact is the image started moving! and it changes color as the mouse change it's location. In the inicial painting, the various concentric circles represent the light shining on the bhuddhas. The antient people drew them overlapping eachother in order to make them vivid. Now, is a more high-technology way, Javascript enabled the picture to make it more interactive, making the scene of the wall painting more vivid, enhansing the "holy" of the image.
  5. ·I believed that the original pattern of the Dunhuang wall paintings were suposed to move, and the reason why it is not moving is because during that time the technology cannot allow the image to move. The pattern during the antient times were made for the Buddhas, while this pattern, with collaburation with the javascript, helped the modern people to understand the cculture of buddhism better.

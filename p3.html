CCLab assignment #3 Experimental Clock

Description

This sketch is a moving image of a clock that can interact with the mouse. When the mouse appeared on the picture, the background of the sketch will change, as if the clock on the background is moving. The circles moving behind it's body are 'minutes' and 'seconds' while the tiny purple shape appeared between it's eyes is 'hours'. This sketch is done by P5 Javascript.

Design Process

Basically this piece is a moving clock, and the figure in the center is the "Time" it self. I created this devil-like figure as the repressentation of time is because time never waits for people, it always consider everyone equally and never agree to give them more times to spend. It's so impartial that sometimes it looks a bit scary. the color of green is influenced by a horror story that I read when I was a child: GOOSEBUMPS HORRORLAND THROW BLANKET. The covor of the book is a monster which look like insects and goats. It have greenish color and purple linings.

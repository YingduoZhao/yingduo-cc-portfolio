CCLab assignment #6 Data Portrait

Description

This sketch is a design of data analize from my sketch book. I have a habbit of doing sketches and draings. Everytime when I got inspirations, or I see some interesting things/experience something interesting, I will write them down/ record them in my sketch book. This sketch book which is been analzed is my 6th sketch book. I started to use it when I started my first day in colledge.

The data analzed in the picture is the compositions inside the sketch book. Since I alften carry it around, I pot a lot of wierd things inside my sketch book, For examples, drawings, writtings, stickers,napkin papers, candy papers, plants, andflowers. I want to know what are the compositions of them,based on pages, are included in my sketh book.

I counted the things one by one, and came up with this data as showed on the previous picture.

Design Process

The above image is the process of my design. I counted the different elements that take part in my sketch books(pencle sketches, water colors, special papers, ink pens sketches, writtings...)

After analyzing the data, I visualized the data by using a histogram.

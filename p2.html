CCLab assignment #2 face generator

Description

This sketch is about a polar bear hinding behind a ice cube on the snow. While thinking realy hard of what to do with the assignment, my brain came up with a recall of a video that I wached recently- a baby polar bear being scared by a baby seal. While noticing giving animals simple-shaped-body and simple-shaped heads makes them cuter, my idea is to draw a cute polar bear. My itention is to make the ice cube change it's color randomly, and the polarbear pop it's head up from the cube if the mouse moves inside the ice cube. However, I only found out how to make the cube change it's color, and didn't find out how to combine the mouse click and the changement (and the poping out function).

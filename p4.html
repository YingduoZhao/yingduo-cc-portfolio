CCLab assignment #4 Exquisite Corpse

Description

This sketch is made of 3 different parts from my group members. During the design, everyone were assigned to make a sketch consists of a eadm a body and legs. After making our sketches, the group rearrange everone's sketches and change them into new sketches. My finished piece is a rabbit-head-corpse with bload flewing out from their body. This sketch is done by P5 Javascript.

Design Process

/>

The design is inspired by the movie Pet Sematary filmed based on the novel ‘Pet Sematary' written by Stephen Edwin King. The movie talkes a bout a family being influenced by the evil energy from the pet graveyard near their house.

I received a rabbit head, a human body wearing a T-shirt and a Kaptain Hook's legs from my group mates. They reminded me of the children wearing animal-head-masks walking in the pet graveyard in the movie.

I tried to make them look creepy by adding blood flows, black-colored-tears and an enlarging red circle that represent bleeding.

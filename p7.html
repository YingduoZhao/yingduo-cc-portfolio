CCLab assignment #7 Autobiographical Game

Description

Every one of us has a story, a narrative that we either tell to ourselves or others, about who we are. This story can reveal a lot about our interests, values, and history.

This design is about my experience of playing in the foreigh sea. When I was a tenager, my family always took me to travel to places, especially beaches. We been to tailand, entering the woods on an elefhant's head, ordering drinks in the underwayter restaurant, looking at the shadows of fishes fly through the ceiling. We been to Hawaii, picking up coral pieces in the wave of Oahu, throw them high into the air. We counted species of fishes in the beach of the Philippines, soaking ourselves in the roof pool of Marina Gold Sands in Singapore.

As the time moves on, I'm 19 now. I will be graduate after 2 and a half years, and startes to exist as a grown up instead of someone's spoiled daughter. My privilege of being a non-responsible spoiled princess is vanishing, and I still didn't get prepaired to become an adult yet. I went to the Yangzi river. It was the clsest place to sea in my home town. I saw night herons sitting on the bench, stairing at the water for foods to eat. I saw sea gulls floating in the sky. I saw dead fishes floating on the water with their belly poniting towards the sky, and the cold and moist wind blowing my hair. Sometimes I wish I could stay in the summer of 2018 forever, standing by the sea, feeling the wave stroking my feed in rhythem like soft winds, stairing at the horizon until the sun sets. And when the stars climed up high in the sky, I would pull my chair to the balcony with my family, looking at them as if they were fireworks given by the gods in heaven. And the time stopped moving, the whole world become scient. All I see and all I can think of, is the movement of the sea and someone surfing in the water through the rhythem of the sea. We are all surfers, and that's why I love the sea.

The main character of the game is the surfer appeared on the center of the picture, the little ball. The reason why I use this shape to represent the main character is that I want to create a blurry imag of the past. Normal people cannot remenber a scene that they saw a few years ago, and I tried to express the feeling of memories by using abstract shapes since it's really difficult for me to remember the actuall look of the surfer. I also put somee previous designs of the character in tthe following picture.

The scene of the game is the image that I saw at Hawii - under the big blue sky, a surfer is surfing in the sea. They need to control their board to avoid themselves from falliing into the water, while the player needs to control the board to keep the surfer from falling.
